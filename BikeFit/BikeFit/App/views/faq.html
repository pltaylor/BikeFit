What is this?
This is a simple web site that is meant to compare different bikes in a simple and visual way.
Where did the data come from?
Up to now the data has come primarily from the manufacturers web sites. If there are inconsistencies in the data it is because I had to calculate the data from other measurements provided by the manufacturers.
Why is my favorite bike company or model not included?
Up to now I have been the only one putting in data to fill this out. If you would like a specific manufacturer or model added please Email Me with the details for all the available sizes and I will try to add it as quickly as possible.
What is the coordinate system?
The ground plane is the zero in Y direction and the center of the bottom bracket is the zero in the X direction. All the bikes should have their bottom brackets lined up left to right.
What are the definitions of the various terms?
Stack:
The vertical distance measured from the center of the bottom bracket to the top of the headtube.
Reach:
The horizontal distance measured from the center of the bottom bracket to the top of the headtube.
Front Center:
The distance measured from the center of the bottom bracket to the center of the front wheel.
Rear Center:
The distance measured from the center of the bottom bracket to the center of the rear wheel.
Head Tube:
The length of the head tube.
